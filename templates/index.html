STUDENT LEAVE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A student leave management system enables students to request time off and allows the SSO to approve those requests. The fact that the majority of pupils have attendance shortages is a common issue in the college.

Apply your leave from anywhere!

Without any issues, you can easily apply for the leave from anywhere. At your own time apply for the leave and get get yourself to save time. 

Types of Leave


Sick


This is a leave where if you are sick and unable to attend the class you can apply to the system for granting a leave.

Medical


This is a leave where incase you want to have a leave for medical checkups or treatment.

Escort


This is a leave where incase you are going as an escort for someone who is sick.

Others


This leave can be applied for if you want to get a leave for other than the above mentioned type of leaves.
